About Ilyas

Storyteller.
Creator. Journalist.

Mass Communications student at Republic Polytechnic with a passion for journalism, video production, and stories that actually matter.

I’m Ilyas, a storyteller driven by curiosity and a genuine belief that journalism can change how people see the world. I specialise in video production and reporting, crafting narratives that inform, engage, and stick. Every story I take on starts with a question I can’t stop thinking about, and ends only when I’m satisfied the audience gets something real from it.

“Stories should matter. If they don’t, why tell them?”

I thrive in fast-paced environments, adapt quickly, and never stop learning. That looks different depending on the week: picking up a new editing technique the night before a deadline, rethinking a shoot when the location falls through, or sitting with a difficult subject long enough to earn their trust. The pressure doesn’t slow me down. If anything, it sharpens my focus.

My work is guided by a commitment to integrity and quality. I don’t believe in filler. A two-minute piece that earns someone’s full attention is worth more than ten minutes of noise. Whether I’m reporting in the field, cutting a sequence in the edit suite, or writing a caption, the standard stays the same: clear, honest, and worth the audience’s time.

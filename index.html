About Ilyas

Where strategy
meets story.

I'm Ilyas Muzaffar — a communications professional and writer who believes the most powerful tool in any organisation's arsenal is a well-told truth.

With a background spanning journalism, public relations, and digital media, I bring a multidisciplinary lens to every brief. Whether shaping a brand narrative, running a crisis communications strategy, or writing a long-form essay — the goal is always the same: clarity, resonance, and trust.

I've worked across industries — from climate tech startups to established nonprofits — helping organisations find and amplify their authentic voice in an increasingly fragmented media landscape.

My approach is grounded in a belief that audiences are not targets. They're people. The moment a brand forgets that is the moment the message is lost.

When I'm not strategising or writing, I'm reading long-form journalism, exploring cities with a camera, or finding the stories most people walk past.
